Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is about web design & development (WordPress, Wix, ClickFunnels, etc.), SEO (Search Engine Optimisation), paid media (Facebook, Instagram, Google AdWords) and video advertising (YouTube).

For each channel, there are different targeting methods. These channels also have different audiences.

The popularity of digital marketing among many brands isn’t a surprise.

Today, effective digital marketing is about connecting with your audience at right time and place.

Take Google AdWords.

You can use it to find potential customers who are seeking to buy a product by showing them search or shopping adverts.

With Google display and video ads, you can also generate awareness.

On the other hand, Facebook advertising provides to target people by psychographics, demographics and online browsing behaviours.

To generate interest, awareness, and desire for a product or service you can benefit from Facebook ads.
